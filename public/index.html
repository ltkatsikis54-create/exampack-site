About

Why I'm building this.

I built ExamPack AI because I've been the student staring at a huge pile of lecture slides before an exam, knowing the content but not having a clean way to study it.

I've been the student who missed a few days of class and got stuck with messy, vague notes from a friend. This is the motive behind this tool. I'm combining the newest AI and development with real evidence-based ways to learn.
